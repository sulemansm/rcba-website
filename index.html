Rotaract Club of Bombay Airport

District 3141 · Rotary International

We started as a small group of passionate young Mumbaikars who believed action speaks louder than words. Today, we're a thriving community of change-makers — students, professionals, dreamers — united by a single purpose.

From planting trees to mentoring children, from blood donation drives to mental health workshops — every project is a chapter in our love story with Bombay.
